About Russian Ballet Graded System

The Russian ballet training system, established by the Moscow State Academy of Choreography, provides a comprehensive and systematic approach to classical ballet education. Grade 1 marks an important milestone where dancers transition from foundational training to more advanced intermediate work, preparing for the rigorous demands of professional-level technique.
