The Essence of Dance - Workshop with Rainer Behr

Workshop Overview

An extraordinary professional development opportunity to learn from Rainer Behr, a distinguished contemporary dancer from the world-renowned Pina Bausch Tanztheater Wuppertal. This intensive workshop, titled "The Essence of Dance," was organized by Goethe-Institut Colombo and provided deep insights into the revolutionary Tanztheater movement and contemporary dance philosophy.

About Rainer Behr

Rainer Behr is a professional contemporary dancer and long-time member of Pina Bausch's legendary dance company, one of the most influential dance theaters in the world. His expertise in Tanztheater—a unique German dance-theater form that combines dance, theater, and emotion—offers a rare glimpse into the artistic legacy of Pina Bausch.

Workshop Experience

The workshop explored fundamental principles of contemporary dance, focusing on authentic movement, emotional expression, and the unique approach to dance-theater that Pina Bausch pioneered. Participants engaged in intensive movement exercises, improvisation techniques, and learned about the philosophy that distinguishes Tanztheater from traditional dance forms.

Through practical exercises and discussions, the workshop emphasized the importance of personal expression in dance, encouraging dancers to find their authentic voice through movement. This approach challenged conventional dance training and opened new perspectives on performance and choreography.

Key Learnings

This workshop provided invaluable exposure to international dance standards and contemporary techniques that are rarely accessible in Sri Lanka. The experience deepened understanding of:

  • • Tanztheater methodology and philosophy
  • • Contemporary dance improvisation techniques
  • • Emotional authenticity in performance
  • • Integration of theater and dance elements
  • • Personal movement exploration and development

Professional Impact

Working with an artist from Pina Bausch Dance Theatre represents a significant milestone in professional development. The insights gained from this workshop continue to influence artistic practice and approach to contemporary dance, providing inspiration and new perspectives on creative expression through movement.
