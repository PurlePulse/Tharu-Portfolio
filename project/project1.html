Swan Lake - Pas de Trois

Project Overview

A performance of the Pas de Trois from Swan Lake. Tharushika danced the lead role, showcasing precision and grace. This project demonstrated a high level of skill and dedication to the art of ballet.

My Role

As the lead dancer, I was responsible for executing the choreography with precision and artistry, as well as conveying the emotions and narrative of the piece to the audience.

Techniques Used

Complex partnering work, interpretive movement, precise footwork, classical ballet technique. A strong focus was placed on both the technical aspects of the dance and the emotional expression.

Behind the Scenes

The rehearsal process involved countless hours of practice, working closely with the choreographer and fellow dancers. We focused on refining our technique, building trust and communication within the group, and understanding the story we were telling. Overcoming the challenges of complex partnering work made the final performance even more rewarding.
